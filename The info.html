Step 1: Your Emulator

The first step is choosing the Snes emulator you want to use. There are many options, such as Zsnes, Bsnes and Bizhawk. However, one of the most trusted and widely used emulators is Snes9x.

It is tried and true, as well as widely available for a casual audience, while emulators such as Bizhawk favor speedrunners. Therefore, for the purpose of this tutorial, this will be our emulator of choise.

The original link to Snes9x doesn't exist anymore. However, many archives have been compiled on it's home page. Anything goes, so simply click on one of those links, and follow the instructions from there! We recommend that you use emulator zone, as it's most easily aproachable for the eyes, and is a simple one-click download.

Once the download completes, simply unzip the package, and your emulator should be ready to go!
