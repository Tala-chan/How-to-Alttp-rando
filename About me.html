About this site

Greetings traveler! The names Tala, and I'm the creator of this site? Why did I make it? For school of course! However, that doesn't mean I don't care for the topic. I was given a choise as to what I want to make, and this is what I decided on. Because in the end, I love emulation and randomizers!

The purpose of this site is to introduce any likeminded individuals to this world. The more the merrier, especially if it makes people happier!

However, if you're on the fence, allow me to share what made me want to get into it in the first place - a youtube channel called SpeedGaming.

Every day or so, Speedgaming releases many new speedruns. Some are daily rando races, and some are even tournaments! The channel is mostly dead, but I was intrigued by the strong sense of passion in the videos. Eventually, I even learned to recognise the narrators!

In the end, I wanted to particiapte in it somehow, and am very grateful I did. Playing has made my days happier, and I only have SpeedGaming to thank for it. If nothing else, I want to give them a special thanks for introducing me, and share their content and interest with the world!

Are you one of the brave adventurers I'm searching for? Well, I hope you feel a sense of companionship in this part of gaming! After all, even if it seems like it, none of us are alone!

Thank you for visitiing this site! I hope it gives you something of value! :)
